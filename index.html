About Sliding Tic-Tac-Toe

Sliding Tic-Tac-Toe (also known as Terni Lapilli) is an ancient Roman board game that adds strategic depth to the classic tic-tac-toe.

Why This Game?

Traditional tic-tac-toe often ends in a draw when both players know the strategy. Sliding Tic-Tac-Toe solves this by adding a movement phase, making every game dynamic and exciting!

History

Terni Lapilli was played in ancient Rome over 2,000 years ago. Archaeological findings show game boards carved into Roman monuments and amphitheater steps.

Strategy Tips

  • Control the center square - it gives you the most movement options
  • Think ahead - plan your moves during the placing phase
  • Create multiple threats - force your opponent into defensive positions
  • Watch for patterns - recognize winning configurations early
